Introduction

Singapore is one of Asia's most prosperous city-states, a global financial hub with an extraordinarily diverse multicultural population and among the world's best infrastructure. Known for Gardens by the Bay, Sentosa Island, hawker centre food culture, Marina Bay Sands and Changi Airport (consistently ranked the world's best). Singapore Changi Airport (SIN) serves the entire region. The Immigration and Checkpoints Authority (ICA) manages all entry and visa matters.

Singapore is one of the most visa-liberal destinations in Asia: citizens of most Western countries, Japan, South Korea, Hong Kong, most ASEAN nations, and many others receive 30–90 days visa-free entry as a Short Visit Pass. For nationalities requiring a visa, Singapore offers an efficient online visa application system.

Tourist & Short Stay

Most nationalities receive a Short Visit Pass (SVP) of 30 days (extendable to 90 days in-country) at immigration. USA, UK, Canada, Australia, EU nations, Japan and many others receive 90 days automatically. No prior application needed for visa-free nationals.

Nationals requiring a Singapore visa must apply online through the ICA e-Visa system or through an authorized agent in Singapore (hotel, tour operator, or Singapore Airlines). The fee is SGD $30 (~USD $22). Processing typically takes 3–5 business days.

Business Visits

Business visits (meetings, conferences) are covered by the Short Visit Pass for most nationalities. Employment in Singapore requires an Employment Pass (EP), S Pass or Work Permit — all administered by the Ministry of Manpower (MOM).

Work & Long-Stay

  • Employment Pass (EP) — for professionals, managers and executives earning SGD $5,000+/month (SGD $5,500+ for financial services). Requires a job offer from a Singapore employer.
  • EntrePass — for innovative entrepreneurs founding startups in Singapore. Requires a recommendation from a Singapore government agency or significant investment/IP.
  • ONE Pass (Overseas Networks and Expertise Pass) — top-tier pass for outstanding global talent earning SGD $30,000+/month; no prior job offer needed; 5-year pass.
  • Tech.Pass — for established tech experts and entrepreneurs; valid 2 years without needing a job offer; salary or business revenue threshold SGD $22,500+/month.
  • Personalised Employment Pass (PEP) — for high-earning EP holders or overseas professionals; not tied to a specific employer.
  • Long Term Visit Pass (LTVP) — for dependants of Singapore PRs or citizens; allows stay of 1–2 years.

Student Pass

International students attending Singapore's full-time schools, polytechnics, or universities need a Student Pass from ICA. The institution typically applies on the student's behalf before arrival. Required: acceptance letter, proof of financial means, and health declaration. Singapore's top universities (NUS, NTU, SMU) are globally ranked.

Airport Transit

Singapore has an exceptional Changi Airport with world-class transit facilities. Most nationalities can transit airside without a visa. Nationals of a few countries require a Visa on Arrival for transit. Check the list at ica.gov.sg.

Required Documents (Singapore Visa)

  • Completed online visa application
  • Valid passport (6+ months validity)
  • Passport-sized photo
  • Return or onward flight bookings
  • Proof of accommodation
  • Bank statements (proof of funds)
  • Supporting documents (employment, enrollment, invitation)
  • SGD $30 visa fee

How to Apply

  1. Check if you need a visa at ica.gov.sg
  2. For visa: apply via ICA's e-Visa portal or through an authorized Singapore agent
  3. Upload required documents and pay SGD $30 fee
  4. Receive visa decision within 3–5 business days
  5. On arrival, the Short Visit Pass duration is stamped in your passport

Who Is Visa-Exempt?

Citizens of USA, UK, Canada, Australia, New Zealand, Japan, South Korea, EU member states, most ASEAN nations, and many others receive visa-free entry for 30–90 days. Full list at ica.gov.sg.

Always verify current visa rules on official government sites before travel.
