About eVisa-Card.com

Your Trusted Visa Guide

Accurate eVisa Information for Global Travelers

eVisa-Card.com is an independent travel information platform dedicated to helping travelers understand visa requirements, online application processes and travel authorizations for countries around the world.

We compile and organize official eVisa information — including visa types, durations, fees, eligibility criteria and step-by-step application guides — so you can plan your trip with confidence. Our goal is to make international travel more accessible by providing clear, up-to-date visa guidance.

Our Mission

Simplifying Visa Information for Every Traveler

Navigating visa requirements can be confusing. eVisa-Card.com was created to remove that uncertainty. We gather official eVisa data from government immigration portals and present it in a clear, structured format so that any traveler can quickly find the information they need.

Whether you need a Schengen visa for Europe, an eVisa for Asia, or a travel authorization for the Americas, our country guides give you a clear starting point before you visit any official government portal to apply.
