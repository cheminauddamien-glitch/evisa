🏥 Health & Entry Requirements for Venezuela

Yellow Fever vaccination recommended for some areas. Medical facilities limited. Travel insurance essential. Check current travel advisories - political situation may affect travel.

🕑 Visa Extension & Overstay Rules in Venezuela

90-day stay may be extended at SAIME (immigration). Overstay: fine, potential deportation.

❌ Visa Refusal & Appeals for Venezuela

May occur based on nationality, political situation. Check current travel advisories. Embassy appointments may be difficult to obtain.
