Indonesia eVOA for Mexican Citizens

Mexican passport holders can enter Indonesia using the Electronic Visa on Arrival (eVOA). The fee is IDR 500,000 (~USD 32), the visa is valid for 30 days, and it can be extended once for a further 30 days at an Indonesian Immigration office.

eVOA Key Details

  • Fee: IDR 500,000 (~USD 32)
  • Validity: 30 days from entry
  • Extension: Once, for 30 additional days (total 60 days)
  • Entries: Single entry
  • Purpose: Tourism / short-stay

How to Apply for the Indonesia eVOA

  1. Visit molina.imigrasi.go.id.
  2. Complete the online eVOA application form with your passport details.
  3. Upload a passport photo and the passport biographical page.
  4. Pay IDR 500,000 (~USD 32) by credit/debit card.
  5. Receive an approval e-mail with a QR code (usually within minutes to 24 hours).
  6. Print or save the approval letter and present it at immigration on arrival.

Required Documents

  • Valid passport (minimum 6 months validity beyond intended stay)
  • Passport-size photo (digital)
  • Return or onward ticket
  • Proof of sufficient funds
  • Accommodation booking

Entry Points

The eVOA is accepted at all major international airports (Soekarno-Hatta, Ngurah Rai/Bali, Juanda, Kualanamu, etc.) and major seaports.
