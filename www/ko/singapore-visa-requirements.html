Singapore eVisa Requirements 2026: Documents, Eligibility & How to Apply

Planning to visit Singapore in 2026? This guide covers every document you need, who is eligible, and the exact steps to apply for a Singapore Visa-free / Tourist Visa / Employment Pass.

Overview: Singapore Visa & Entry Requirements 2026

Visa-free for 160+ nationalities including EU, UK, US, Australia. The Visa-free / Tourist Visa / Employment Pass system makes Singapore highly accessible — apply online at mom.gov.sg.

The official fee is SGD 30 (~USD 22) for tourist visa; visa-free for most with a standard processing time of 3–8 weeks (Employment Pass). The permitted stay is up to 30 days visa-free (most nationalities). Entry is Multiple entry (visa-free). Currency in use: SGD (Singapore Dollar).

Who Is Eligible for a Singapore Visa?

Eligibility: Visa-free for 160+ countries; visa required for ~30 nationalities.

Special note: Visa-free for 160+ nationalities including EU, UK, US, Australia.

Children under 18 must travel with a valid passport of their own. Minors travelling without both parents may need a notarised parental consent letter. Always verify current rules at the official Singapore immigration authority before travel.

How to Apply: Step-by-Step for Singapore in 2026

  1. Check your visa-free status — Visit ica.gov.sg to confirm whether your nationality enjoys visa-free access to Singapore for 30–90 days. Over 160 nationalities qualify.
  2. Complete the Singapore Arrival Card — All visitors must complete the Singapore Arrival Card (SGAC) free at ica.gov.sg/arrival within 3 days before arrival, regardless of visa status.
  3. Prepare required documents — Have a valid passport (6+ months), return ticket, hotel booking, and bank statements or credit card showing sufficient funds (SGD 100/day recommended).
  4. Apply for a visa if required — If your nationality requires a visa, apply through the ICA e-service, Singapore embassy, or accredited travel agent, paying SGD 30 (~USD 22).
  5. Present at Singapore immigration — At the border, present your passport and SGAC confirmation to the immigration officer for instant clearance (visa-free) or visa stamp.

Apply via the official portal: mom.gov.sg. Avoid third-party agents who charge unnecessary service fees on top of the official government fee.

건강 및 입국 요건

  • Medical exam: Required for all work pass holders (EP, S Pass, Work Permit). Includes HIV test, syphilis screening, malaria, chest X-ray, and pregnancy test for female WP holders. Must be performed by a registered Singapore doctor.
  • 6-monthly medical exam: Work Permit holders in certain sectors require medical exams every 6 months.
  • Vaccinations: Yellow fever certificate required if arriving from an endemic area. No other mandatory vaccinations for entry.
  • COVID-19: No vaccination or testing requirements as of 2026.
  • Health insurance: Employers are required to provide medical insurance for Work Permit and S Pass holders (minimum SGD 15,000 annual coverage).
  • Biometrics: Fingerprints and photo collected at immigration checkpoint for all visitors.

비자 연장 및 체류 초과

  • Tourist visa / visa-free: May be extended online through ICA e-Service for up to 89 days total. Fee: SGD 40 per extension. Approval is discretionary.
  • Employment Pass: Renewable in 2-3 year increments. Apply at least 3 months before expiry through EP Online. Must continue to meet salary and COMPASS requirements.
  • S Pass / Work Permit: Renewable by employer. Work Permits have maximum employment periods by nationality and sector.
  • Student Pass: Renewable with continued enrollment.
  • Overstay: Criminal offense in Singapore. Penalties include imprisonment (up to 6 months), fine, caning (for males under 50, if illegal entry), and deportation with re-entry ban.

거부 및 항소

  • Common refusal reasons: Insufficient documentation, security concerns, previous immigration violations, employer quota exceeded (S Pass/WP), failed COMPASS criteria (EP), salary below threshold, false information.
  • Appeal: EP/S Pass refusals can be appealed within 3 months through EP Online with additional supporting documents. Tourist visa refusals have no formal appeal.
  • Reapplication: Can reapply with improved documentation. Multiple consecutive rejections may trigger additional scrutiny.
