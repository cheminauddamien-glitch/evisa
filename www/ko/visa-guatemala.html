Visa-Free Entry

Most nationalities can enter Guatemala visa-free for up to 90 days (part of the CA-4 agreement with Honduras, El Salvador, and Nicaragua).

Guatemala, home to ancient Maya temples and stunning Lake Atitlán, allows most nationalities to enter visa-free for 90 days under the CA-4 agreement (Guatemala, Honduras, El Salvador, Nicaragua).
