Visa-Free Entry to Indonesia for Singaporean Citizens

Singaporean passport holders can enter Indonesia visa-free for up to 30 days under the ASEAN agreement. No visa application or fee is required — simply present a valid passport on arrival.

Entry Requirements

  • Valid passport (minimum 6 months validity beyond intended stay)
  • Return or onward ticket
  • Proof of sufficient funds
  • Accommodation booking (may be requested)

Important Notes

  • The 30-day visa-free stay cannot be extended under this category.
  • For longer stays, apply for an eVOA or social/business visa before travel.
  • Entry is permitted through all major international airports and seaports.
