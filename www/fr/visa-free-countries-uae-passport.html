UAE Passport Pays sans visa 2026 — 180+ Destinations

The UAE passport is one of the world's fastest-rising travel documents, granting access to 180+ countries visa-free or with simple online authorisation in 2026. The UAE passport has risen dramatically over the past decade through aggressive diplomatic engagement, securing visa-waiver deals with the EU Schengen Area, UK, Russia, China, Brazil, and most of Africa and Asia. UAE citizens can visit all 27 EU Schengen countries, the UK, Japan, South Korea, and the vast majority of Africa without a visa. Its ranking reflects the UAE's success as a neutral, globally-connected nation despite the lack of US Visa Waiver Program membership.

Rising Passport: The UAE passport rose from rank ~65 in 2014 to the global top 15 by 2026 — one of the fastest improvements in passport history.

Pays nécessitant un visa

UAE passport holders need advance visas for: USA (B1/B2 visa), Canada, and a small number of nations. Despite not being in the US Visa Waiver Program, UAE citizens with strong financial profiles typically receive US visas. The UAE also benefits from strong relationships with regional powers, making Gulf travel entirely unrestricted.
