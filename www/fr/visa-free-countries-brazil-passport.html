Brazil Passport Pays sans visa 2026 — 171+ Destinations

The Brazilian passport grants access to 171+ countries visa-free or with simple online authorisation in 2026. Brazil's passport strength comes from Mercosur agreements giving unrestricted access across all of South America, plus the EU-Brazil bilateral agreement covering all 27 Schengen nations. In a major upgrade, Brazil joined the US Visa Waiver Program in 2024, meaning Brazilians can now visit the USA with just an ESTA. Portugal, due to shared language and historic ties, offers particularly warm reception, and Brazil's passport also opens doors across Africa, the Caribbean, and parts of Southeast Asia.

2024 Milestone: Brazil joined the US Visa Waiver Program — Brazilians can now visit the USA with ESTA ($21), replacing the previous B1/B2 visa requirement.

Pays nécessitant un visa

Brazilians need advance visas for: China, Russia, India, Japan, Canada, Australia, and some Central Asian and Pacific nations. Japan requires a tourist visa for Brazilians, unlike most Western passport holders.
