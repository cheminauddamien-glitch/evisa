Cambodge eVisa Processing Time

eVisas applied for at evisa.gov.kh are processed in approximately 3 business days. Applicants receive an email with the approved eVisa PDF for printing. During Cambodian public holidays (Khmer New Year in April, Pchum Ben, etc.) processing may be slower. Apply at least 7 days in advance to leave room for any delays or document correction requests.

Visa on Arrival Processing

The VOA at Phnom Penh and Siem Reap airports typically takes 15–45 minutes depending on queue length. At busy international airports, have your passport, photo, USD 35 in cash, and accommodation details ready. Land border crossings can take 30–90 minutes. Having all documents ready reduces waiting time significantly.

Tips to Speed Up Cambodge Visa Processing

Apply for the eVisa at evisa.gov.kh at least 7 days before departure to allow for the 3-business-day processing window plus buffer time. Ensure your photo meets the 4×6cm white-background requirement. For VOA, arrive with the exact USD 35 in crisp notes (some counters are strict about this). Avoid crossing at smaller, less-staffed land borders during peak public holiday periods.

Delays & Common Issues

  • Photo does not meet the 4×6cm or white background requirement
  • Passport expiry within 6 months of arrival
  • System downtime on the evisa.gov.kh portal
  • Incorrect passport number or date of birth on eVisa application
  • Insufficient USD cash for VOA payment
  • Peak season crowds at airports (December–January, April for Khmer New Year)
