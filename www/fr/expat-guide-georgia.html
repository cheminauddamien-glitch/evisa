Guide Expatriation : Géorgie 2026

La Géougie (le pays du Caucase) est devenue l'une des le plus populaire destinations for digital nomads, entrepreneurs et expats worldwide. The reasons: 365-day visa-free entry for 95+ nationalities, a flat 20% income tax (1% for small businesses), ultra-low cost of living, fast internet et a young, cosmopolitan capital in Tbilisi.

🛂 Visa & Options de Résidence

Type de VisaDétails
Visa-Free Stay (365 days)Les citoyens de 95+ pays (y compris USA, EU, UK, Canada, Australia) peuvent séjourner 365 jours consécutifs sans a visa. Pas de condition de revenus ni d'investissement. L'option la plus simple pour les nomades numériques.
Residency by InvestmentInvestment in Georgian real estate or business. Minimum $100,000 in real estate OR $300,000 in business. Temporary residency for 6 years, then permanent.
Employment ResidencyFor those employed by a Georgian company. Employer applies on your behalf. Temporary residency.
Permanent ResidencyAfter 6 years of continuous legal residence. Or by marriage to a Georgian citizen (3 years).
Citizenship by InvestmentExceptional status granted by Presidential decree. Requires significant economic contribution. Rare but possible.

Processus Étape par Étape

  1. Les citoyens de 95+ pays: simply enter Georgia with a valid passport — no pre-arrival visa needed
  2. Register your stay at the House of Justice (სამართლის სახლი) if staying 30+ days
  3. Obtain a Personal Identification Number (personal ID) at the Public Service Hall
  4. Ouvrez un Georgian bank account (nécessite passport + Georgian numéro de téléphone)
  5. Register for tax purposes at the Revenue Service (rs.ge) if earning Georgian income
  6. Pour la résidence : faites votre demande à l'Agence du Registre Civil avec preuve d'investissement
💡 Conseil Pro : Georgia's 1% flat tax on annual revenue up to 500,000 GEL (~$180,000) for small status businesses makes it extraordinarily attractive for freelancers et entrepreneurs. Register as a 'Small Business' (მცირე ბიზნესი) or 'Virtual Zone Company' for IT businesses (0% coupouate tax, 5% dividend tax).

🏥 Santé en Géougie

Santé Publique

Georgia has a Universal Healthcare programme (UHC) that fournit basic coverage to all Georgian citizens et legal residents. Foreigners sans residency are not covered. Emergency treatment est disponible to all, but costs doit être paid privately.

Santé Privée

Hôpital privés in Tbilisi are modern et affordable: Mediclub Georgia, Todua Medical Centre, Acad. O. Gudushauri Hospital, American Medical Center. Quality varies — major hospitals in Tbilisi are good; rural areas have limited facilities.

ℹ️ Recommandé : Healthcare is very cheap in Georgia. Even sans assurance, paying out of pocket for soins courants is affordable. International assurance santé is recommandé primarily for serious illness, surgery or évacuation médicale.

🛡️ Assurance Santé en Géougie

L'assurance santé n'est pas requise pour les séjours sans visa en Géorgie. Cependant, elle est fortement recommandée compte tenu des limites du système de santé public et du besoin de couverture d'évacuation médicale for serious conditions.

Meilleurs Assureurs pour Expatriés

GPI Insurance (Georgia)
Georgia's largest local insurer. Affoudable plans from ~80 GEL/mois. Good network of local hospitals.
Aldagi (Vienna Insurance Group)
Strong local insurer. Formules complètes with dentaire. À partir de ~100 GEL/mois.
Imedi L (local)
Bon rapport qualité-prix for soins courants. Populaire auprès de local expat community. À partir de ~70 GEL/mois.
Cigna Global
International plan for worldwide coverage y compris évacuation médicale. À partir de ~80 $/mois.
SafetyWing
Nomad-focused insurance. Affoudable, flexible. Bon pour shout-to-medium stays. À partir de ~45 $/mois.
💡 Conseil Pro : SafetyWing is populaire auprès de the large digital nomad community in Tbilisi — very affordable et easy to sign up for. For longer-term residents, a local Georgian plan (GPI, Aldagi) fournit better coverage at lower cost.

🏦 Ouvrir un Compte Bancaire en Géougie

Georgia has one of the most foreigner-friendly banking environments in the world. Bank accounts can be opened quickly, souvent same-day, with just a passport — no residency required.

Banques Recommandées

TBC Bank — Georgia's largest bank. Excellent English app. Fastest account opening (sometimes 15 minutes in-branch). Multi-currency accounts. Highly recommended.
Bank of Georgia — Second-largest bank. Good digital banking. English-speaking staff. Multi-currency (GEL, USD, EUR).
Credo Bank — Bon pour small businesses et entrepreneurs. Taux de change compétitifs.
Liberty Bank — Widespread branches. Bon pour everyday transactions.
Wise / Revolut — Pas des banques géorgiennes mais largement utilisées pour les transferts internationaux. Complétez votre compte local TBC ou Bank of Georgia.

Documents Requis

  • Passeport valide (that's it — no residency requis pour basic accounts)
  • Georgian numéro de téléphone (for 2FA — buy a SIM at the airport for ~$5)
  • In some cases: proof of address (rental contract)

Processus Étape par Étape

  1. Buy a Georgian SIM card (Magti or Geocell) at the airport
  2. Rendez-vous dans n'importe quelle agence TBC Bank ou Bank of Georgia
  3. Present passport et numéro de téléphone
  4. Compte ouvert le jour même — typically within 30–60 minutes
  5. Recevez votre carte de débit immediately or within 1–2 days
  6. Activate mobile banking via the app
💡 Conseil Pro : TBC Bank is the clear expat favourrrite in Georgia — the app is in English, account opening takes 15–30 minutes with just your passport, et you get a multi-currency account (GEL + USD + EUR) from day one. No other country in the world makes banking this easy for foreigners.

🏠 Acheter un Bien Immobilier en Géougie

Georgia has no restrictions on foreign property ownership. Foreigners can buy any type of property — apartments, houses, commercial — with the same rights as Georgian citizens. The market is transparent, prices are low, et the process is quick. Investment ≥$100,000 qualifies for residency.

Options pour les Étrangers

Freehold Purchase
Full ownership. No restrictions. Registered at the National Agency of Public Registry (NAPR). Most secure et straightforward.
Residency Qualification
Investissement immobilier ≥$100,000 qualifies for a 6-year temporary residency permit.
Off-Plan / New Development
Popular in Tbilisi, Batumi et Gudauri. Developers offer flexible plans de paiement. Batumi (Black Sea) is a popular shout-term rental market.

Processus d'Achat

  1. Pas d'avocat strictement requis (le système est très simple) mais fortement recommandéé for foreigners
  2. Agree price with seller — a real estate agent is optional
  3. Visit the House of Justice (სამართლის სახლი) together
  4. Present passports of buyer et seller
  5. Signez le/la purchase agreement et pay
  6. Registration completed on-the-spot or within 1–4 jours ouvrables
  7. Receive new ownership certificate

Coûts Typiques

ÉlémentCoût
Property registration fee~50–200 GEL (~$18–73) — extremely low
Notarisation (if applicable)~100–300 GEL
Commission de l’agent2–5% (if applicable)
Taxe foncière annuelle0.05–1% depending on owner income et property value
No real estate transfer tax—
💡 Conseil Pro : Georgia has one of the world's simplest property purchase systems. Two people walk into a House of Justice, sign a document et walk out with a new ownership certificate — parfois in under an hour. No lawyers, no notaries, no complex vérifications préalables required (though still recommandé). Batumi has a booming shout-term rental market with yields of 8–12%.
