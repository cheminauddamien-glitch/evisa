India Passport Pays sans visa 2026 — 58+ Destinations

The Indian passport provides visa-free or visa-on-arrival access to approximately 58 countries in 2026. While more limited compared to Western passports, Indian citizens benefit from growing bilateral agreements, especially across Southeast Asia, South Asia, Africa, and the Caribbean. Thailand introduced free visa-on-arrival for Indians, and several ASEAN nations offer easy e-Visa access. Indians holding a valid US, UK, Schengen, or Australian visa may also qualify for additional eVisa or visa-free privileges in countries like the UAE, Bahrain, and Oman, significantly expanding travel options beyond the base 58-country count.

Tip: A valid US, UK, or Schengen visa unlocks extra visa-on-arrival access in many Gulf, Southeast Asian, and Latin American countries for Indian passport holders.

Pays nécessitant un visa

Indian passport holders need advance visas for most Western countries: USA (B1/B2 visa), UK, EU/Schengen countries, Canada, Australia, Japan, UAE (advance visa), Russia, and China. These visas are obtainable but require full applications, documentation, and in some cases interviews.
