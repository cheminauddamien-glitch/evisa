Indonesia Visa on Arrival for Chinese Citizens

Chinese passport holders can enter Indonesia using either the Visa on Arrival (VoA) obtained at the airport counter, or the online Electronic Visa on Arrival (eVOA) via molina.imigrasi.go.id. Both cost IDR 500,000 (~USD 32) and allow a 30-day stay.

VoA / eVOA Key Details

  • Fee: IDR 500,000 (~USD 32)
  • Validity: 30 days from entry
  • Extension: Once, for 30 additional days
  • Entries: Single entry
  • Purpose: Tourism / short-stay

How to Apply

Option A — eVOA (recommended, apply before travel)

  1. Visit molina.imigrasi.go.id.
  2. Fill in the application with your passport details and upload a photo.
  3. Pay IDR 500,000 online by card.
  4. Receive approval by e-mail; present QR code on arrival.

Option B — Visa on Arrival counter

  1. Proceed to the VoA counter on arrival at a designated airport or seaport.
  2. Pay IDR 500,000 (cash or card, USD/IDR accepted at most airports).
  3. Collect the visa stamp and proceed to immigration.

Required Documents

  • Valid passport (minimum 6 months validity)
  • Return or onward ticket
  • Proof of accommodation
  • Sufficient funds for the stay
