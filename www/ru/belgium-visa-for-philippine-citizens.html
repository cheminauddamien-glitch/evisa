Philippine Citizens Visa Requirement for Belgium

Philippine passport holders must obtain a Schengen Short-Stay Visa (Type C) before travelling to Belgium. The visa fee is EUR 80, processing takes 15 working days, and applications are submitted through VFS Global or the Belgium embassy in your country.

Schengen Visa Key Details

  • Fee: EUR 80 (adults); EUR 40 (children 6–11); free (under 6)
  • Processing time: 15 working days
  • Max stay: 90 days in any 180-day period
  • Valid across the entire Schengen Area (27 countries)
  • Apply through: VFS Global or the Belgium embassy
  • Biometrics: Required at appointment

How to Apply for a Belgium Schengen Visa

  1. Book an appointment at VFS Global or the Belgium embassy in your country.
  2. Download and complete the Schengen visa application form.
  3. Pay the EUR 80 visa fee at the VFS centre or embassy.
  4. Attend your appointment and provide biometric data (fingerprints and photograph).
  5. Submit all supporting documents (see list below).
  6. Wait 15 working days for a decision. Collect your passport with visa sticker.

Required Documents

  • Valid passport (at least 3 months validity beyond travel dates; 2 blank pages)
  • Two recent passport-sized photos (ICAO standard)
  • Completed Schengen visa application form
  • Travel insurance (minimum EUR 30,000 cover) for the entire Schengen Area
  • Bank statements (last 3–6 months)
  • Proof of accommodation in Belgium (hotel bookings or invitation letter)
  • Round-trip flight reservation
  • Proof of employment, business registration, or student status
