Do Indian Citizens Need a Visa for Thailand?

Indian citizens require a visa to enter Thailand. The most convenient option is the Thailand eVisa, available online at thaievisa.go.th. The Tourist eVisa (TR) allows a 60-day stay and costs THB 2,000 (~$55).

How to Apply

  1. Visit thaievisa.go.th
  2. Create an account and select Tourist Visa (TR)
  3. Upload: passport copy, passport photo, flight booking, accommodation proof, bank statement
  4. Pay THB 2,000 online by card
  5. Receive eVisa PDF by email within 3–5 business days
  6. Print and present at the Thai port of entry

Tips for Indian Travelers

  • Ensure your passport is valid for at least 6 months beyond your intended stay.
  • Carry a printed return or onward ticket — immigration officers may ask for it on arrival.
  • Keep THB 10,000 per person (or equivalent) in accessible funds.
  • Travel insurance is not mandatory but strongly recommended.
  • To extend your stay, visit any Thai Immigration Office (extension fee: THB 1,900 for 30 days).
