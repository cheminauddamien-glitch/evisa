Do US Citizens Need a Visa for Czech Republic?

No. US passport holders can visit Czech Republic visa-free for up to 90 days in any 180-day period across the entire Schengen Area. This covers tourism, family visits, and short business trips.

From 2025, US travellers must register for ETIAS before entering the Schengen Area. The online application costs €7 and is valid for 3 years.

ETIAS for Czech Republic

  • Cost: €7
  • Validity: 3 years (or until passport expiry)
  • Apply: online at the official ETIAS website — approval usually within minutes
  • Required: before boarding any flight/train to a Schengen country

Border Documents

  • Valid US passport (min. 3 months validity beyond planned departure)
  • ETIAS authorisation (from 2025)
  • Return or onward travel ticket
  • Proof of accommodation
  • Proof of sufficient funds (~€100/day recommended)
  • Travel/health insurance
