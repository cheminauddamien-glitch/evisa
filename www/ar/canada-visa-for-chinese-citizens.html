Canadian Visitor Visa for Chinese Citizens

Chinese passport holders require a Temporary Resident Visa (TRV) — commonly called a Canadian visitor visa — to enter Canada. The application fee is CAD 100. Biometrics (fingerprints and photo) are also required (CAD 85). Processing typically takes 4–12 weeks via the IRCC online portal.

How to Apply for a Canadian Visa in 2026

  1. Create an account on ircc.canada.ca.
  2. Complete the online application form (IMM 5257).
  3. Pay the CAD 100 visa fee (and CAD 85 biometrics fee if applicable).
  4. Submit documents electronically.
  5. Attend a biometrics appointment at a Visa Application Centre (VAC).
  6. Await a decision — typically 4–12 weeks.
  7. If approved, your passport will be returned with the visa sticker.

Required Documents

  • Valid Chinese passport
  • Completed IMM 5257 application form
  • Two recent passport photos
  • Proof of financial support (bank statements, last 3–6 months)
  • Travel itinerary and accommodation bookings
  • Letter of invitation (if visiting family/friends)
  • Proof of employment or enrolment (employment letter, pay stubs)
  • Biometrics appointment confirmation

Tips for a Successful Application

IRCC assesses applications based on your ties to your home country (employment, family, property) and the purpose of your visit. Provide complete, honest, and well-organised documentation. Gaps in information or inconsistencies are the leading cause of refusals. Consider using a certified immigration consultant if your case is complex.
