Document Preparation Tips

  1. Organise documents in the order specified by the embassy — it speeds up processing.
  2. Make digital backups of everything before submitting originals.
  3. Certify translations through a sworn translator — free online translations are not accepted.
  4. For eVisa applications, ensure scanned files are clear, under the specified file size, and in the correct format (usually JPEG or PDF).
  5. Apply well in advance — see our processing times guide.
