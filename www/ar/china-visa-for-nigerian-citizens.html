China Visa for Nigerian Citizens

Nigerian passport holders must obtain a standard China visa from the nearest Chinese embassy or consulate before travelling. The standard tourist (L) visa fee is approximately USD 140, and processing takes 4–7 business days. Apply via the Chinese Visa Application Service Centre (CVASC) in your country.

Standard Visa Key Details

  • Fee: approx. USD 140 (confirm with local Chinese embassy)
  • Visa types: Tourist (L), Business (M), Transit (G)
  • Stay: 30–90 days as granted
  • Processing: 4–7 business days (express available at some locations)
  • Apply at: Chinese embassy, consulate, or CVASC

How to Apply

  1. Locate the nearest Chinese embassy, consulate, or Chinese Visa Application Service Centre (CVASC) at visaforchina.cn.
  2. Download and complete the DS-160 equivalent — the China Visa Application Form (V.2013).
  3. Prepare all required documents (see below).
  4. Submit your application and pay the visa fee (approx. USD 140).
  5. Collect your passport with the visa stamp after 4–7 business days.

Required Documents

  • Valid passport — minimum 6 months validity, at least one blank visa page
  • Completed China Visa Application Form (V.2013)
  • Passport photo — 48×33 mm, white background, recent
  • Return or onward flight ticket
  • Hotel booking or invitation letter
  • Bank statements (last 3–6 months)
  • Travel insurance (recommended)
