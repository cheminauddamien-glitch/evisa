Do Indian Citizens Need a Visa for France?

Yes. Indian passport holders must obtain a Schengen Type C visa before travelling to France. There is no eVisa option — applications must be submitted in person at a French consulate or an authorised VFS Global centre in India.

Schengen Visa Requirements

The Schengen visa allows stays of up to 90 days in any 180-day period across all 27 Schengen countries. The adult fee is €80 (non-refundable). Apply at least 3 weeks before your departure date; allow up to 45 days during peak season.

How to Apply

  1. Create an account at france-visas.gouv.fr and complete the application form.
  2. Book an appointment at VFS Global or the French Consulate.
  3. Attend in person, submit documents, and provide biometrics (fingerprints + photo).
  4. Pay the €80 fee. Track your application online.

Required Documents

  • Valid Indian passport (min. 2 blank pages, issued within last 10 years)
  • Two recent colour passport photos (35×45 mm, white background)
  • Completed Schengen visa application form
  • Bank statements — last 3 months (min. ~€120/day of stay)
  • Travel insurance — min. €30,000 coverage across Schengen Area
  • Confirmed return flight itinerary
  • Proof of accommodation (hotel booking or host invitation)
  • Employment letter / leave approval / proof of self-employment
