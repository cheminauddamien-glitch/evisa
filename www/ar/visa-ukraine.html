🏥 Health & Entry Requirements for Ukraine

No mandatory vaccinations. Health insurance recommended. Medical facilities adequate in Kyiv, limited elsewhere. Note: travel advisories may be in effect due to conflict situation. Check government travel warnings before planning travel.

🕑 Visa Extension & Overstay Rules in Ukraine

90-day visa-free not extendable. Type D allows TRP application in Ukraine. Overstay: fine, deportation, 5-year entry ban.

❌ Visa Refusal & Appeals for Ukraine

May occur based on nationality, security concerns, conflict zone proximity. No formal appeal. Check current travel advisories.
