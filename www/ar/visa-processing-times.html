Visa وقت المعالجةs 2026 — How Long Does a Visa Take?

Processing times vary enormously by country, visa type, and your nationality. Use this table to plan ahead — always apply well in advance to avoid delays, especially during peak travel seasons.

Allow extra time: Published processing times are estimates only. Peak seasons (summer, holidays), high application volumes, or missing documents can significantly extend waiting times.

How Far in Advance Should You Apply?

  • eVisa (Turkey, India, Vietnam): At least 2 weeks before travel for safety; many approved in 24–72 hours.
  • USA B-1/B-2 visa: 3–6 months minimum due to interview wait times at many consulates.
  • Schengen visa: Apply 15 calendar days before travel, maximum 6 months before.
  • Australia tourist visa: 6–8 weeks minimum for paper applications; ETA is near-instant.
  • UK Standard Visitor: Apply at least 3 weeks before; allow 6 weeks in peak periods.
