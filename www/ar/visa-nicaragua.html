🏥 Health & Entry Requirements for Nicaragua

No mandatory vaccinations. Yellow Fever certificate if from endemic area. Dengue and Zika present. Medical facilities limited. Travel insurance recommended.

🕑 Visa Extension & Overstay Rules in Nicaragua

CA-4 90-day period shared between Guatemala, Honduras, El Salvador, Nicaragua. Must exit CA-4 region for 72 hours to reset. Overstay fine: $2/day.

❌ Visa Refusal & Appeals for Nicaragua

Rare for tourists. May occur based on nationality. No formal appeal.
