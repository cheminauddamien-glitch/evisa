Brazil eVisa for Indian Citizens

Indians must apply for the Brazil eVisa through the official portal at gov.br. The eVisa costs USD 80 and is processed in Up to 72 hours. It allows a stay of up to 90 days.

How to Apply for the Brazil eVisa

  1. Visit the official Brazil eVisa portal at gov.br.
  2. Create an account and complete the online application form.
  3. Upload required documents (see list below).
  4. Pay the USD 80 application fee by credit/debit card.
  5. Receive your approved eVisa by email within Up to 72 hours.
  6. Print the eVisa approval and carry it when travelling to Brazil.

Required Documents

  • Valid Indian passport (at least 6 months validity beyond departure date)
  • Passport-quality digital photograph (white background)
  • Valid email address for communication
  • Credit or debit card for the USD 80 fee
  • Travel itinerary (flight bookings, accommodation)
  • Bank statements or proof of sufficient funds

eVisa Conditions

  • Fee: USD 80 (non-refundable)
  • Processing: Up to 72 hours
  • Stay: up to 90 days per visit
  • Purpose: tourism, business, transit
  • Multiple entries may be granted

Brazil eVisa Cost for Indian Citizens

The eVisa for Indian travellers costs USD 80 and authorises a stay of 90 days. Apply online at gov.br (official portal) before you travel. Typical processing is Up to 72 hours, so apply with a comfortable margin before departure.

Length of Stay & Overstaying Brazil

The standard tourist stay for Indian citizens is 90 days. Overstaying this limit in Brazil can lead to fines, deportation and a temporary re-entry ban, so track your permitted days carefully. If you need longer, enquire about an extension or a different visa category with Brazil’s immigration authority before your authorised stay expires.
