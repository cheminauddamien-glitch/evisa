Visa-Free Entry for Argentinian Citizens

Argentinian passport holders enjoy visa-free entry to Singapore for up to 30 days. No pre-arranged visa is required; simply present a valid passport, proof of onward travel, and sufficient funds at the Singapore Immigration checkpoint.

Conditions for Entry

  • Valid passport with at least 6 months validity
  • Confirmed onward/return ticket
  • Sufficient funds for the duration of stay
  • Accommodation details (hotel booking or host address)
  • No criminal record that would bar entry

Extending Your Stay

Extensions beyond 30 days may be requested at an ICA service centre before expiry. Approval is at the Immigration & Checkpoints Authority's discretion.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do Argentinian citizens need a visa for Singapore?

No. Argentinian passport holders can enter Singapore visa-free for up to 30 days for tourism and short business visits.

Can I extend my stay in Singapore?

Yes. Visit an ICA service centre before your 30-day allowance expires to apply for an extension.

What documents do I need to enter Singapore visa-free?

You need a valid passport (6+ months), onward/return ticket, accommodation details, and sufficient funds.
