Singapore Visa for Nigerian Citizens

Nigerian passport holders require a visa to enter Singapore. Applications are submitted online through the ICA e-Service portal or via a Singapore embassy. The tourist visa fee is approximately SGD 30 and processing takes 3–5 working days.

How to Apply

  1. Visit ica.gov.sg and access the e-Visa application portal, or contact a Singapore embassy in your country.
  2. Complete the Singapore Tourist Visa application form.
  3. Upload all required supporting documents.
  4. Pay the visa fee of approximately SGD 30.
  5. Await a decision (typically 3–5 working days).

Required Documents

  • Valid passport (minimum 6 months validity)
  • Completed visa application form
  • Recent passport-sized photographs
  • Bank statements (last 3 months)
  • Confirmed return/onward ticket
  • Hotel booking or host invitation letter
  • Travel insurance (recommended)

Frequently Asked Questions

Do Nigerian citizens need a visa for Singapore?

Yes. Nigerian passport holders must obtain a Singapore Tourist Visa before travel. Apply via the ICA e-Service portal at ica.gov.sg.

How much does a Singapore visa cost?

The Singapore tourist visa fee is approximately SGD 30. Additional service charges may apply if applying through an agent.

How long does Singapore visa processing take?

Standard processing takes 3–5 working days. Apply at least 2 weeks before your intended travel date.
