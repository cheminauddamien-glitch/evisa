🏥 Health & Entry Requirements for Luxembourg

Schengen travel insurance EUR 30,000 minimum. No mandatory vaccinations. Luxembourg has excellent public healthcare (CNS system). Residents must join CNS. EHIC for EU citizens.

🕑 Visa Extension & Overstay Rules in Luxembourg

Schengen 90/180 rule. National visa holders register at commune within 3 days. Overstay penalties: fine, deportation, Schengen-wide ban up to 5 years.

❌ Visa Refusal & Appeals for Luxembourg

Standard Schengen refusal procedures. Appeal to Tribunal Administratif within 3 months. High acceptance rate for Luxembourg.
