🏥 Health & Entry Requirements for Myanmar

No mandatory vaccinations but Yellow Fever certificate required from endemic areas. Hepatitis A/B, Typhoid, Japanese Encephalitis recommended. Malaria prophylaxis for rural areas. Medical facilities very limited - evacuation to Bangkok for serious cases. Travel/medical evacuation insurance essential.

🕑 Visa Extension & Overstay Rules in Myanmar

Tourist e-Visa not extendable. Must leave within 28 days. Business visa extendable at immigration in Yangon. Overstay: $3/day fine, detention possible. Must pay fine before departure.

❌ Visa Refusal & Appeals for Myanmar

Political situation may affect visa issuance. Refusals based on nationality restrictions, previous violations. No formal appeal. Check current Myanmar travel advisories before applying.
