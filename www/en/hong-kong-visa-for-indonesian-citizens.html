Visa-Free Entry to Hong Kong for Indonesian Citizens

Indonesian passport holders can enter Hong Kong visa-free for up to 30 days per visit. No prior application is required — simply present your valid passport on arrival at any Hong Kong immigration control point.

Visa-Free Stay Details

  • Stay allowed: Up to 30 days per visit
  • Permitted activities: Tourism, business meetings, transit
  • Work: Not permitted under visa-free entry
  • No eTA or pre-registration required

What to Carry on Arrival

  • Valid Indonesian passport (valid for the full duration of your stay)
  • Return or onward travel ticket
  • Proof of sufficient funds
  • Accommodation details

Extending Your Stay

If you wish to stay longer than 30 days, you must apply for an extension at the Hong Kong Immigration Department (immd.gov.hk) before your permitted period expires.

Cost & Validity for Indonesian Travellers

Indonesian citizens pay no visa fee to enter Hong Kong for tourism. The permitted stay is 30 days per visit, granted automatically on arrival with a valid passport. Because entry is visa-free, there is no application form, no embassy appointment and no processing wait — entry is granted at the border.

Length of Stay & Overstaying Hong Kong

The standard tourist stay for Indonesian citizens is 30 days per visit. Overstaying this limit in Hong Kong can lead to fines, deportation and a temporary re-entry ban, so track your permitted days carefully. If you need longer, enquire about an extension or a different visa category with Hong Kong’s immigration authority before your authorised stay expires.
