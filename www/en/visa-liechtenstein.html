Visa-Free Entry

🏥 Health & Entry Requirements for Liechtenstein

Same as Schengen requirements. Travel health insurance minimum EUR 30,000 coverage mandatory. No specific vaccination requirements. EU/EEA EHIC card accepted for emergency care.

🕑 Visa Extension & Overstay Rules in Liechtenstein

Schengen 90/180 day rule applies. Cannot extend beyond 90 days without residence permit. Overstay: Schengen-wide ban possible. Extremely difficult to obtain long-term residence - population controlled.

❌ Visa Refusal & Appeals for Liechtenstein

Same as Schengen refusal procedures. Apply through Swiss embassy. Appeal through Swiss administrative courts.
