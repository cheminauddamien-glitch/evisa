Australia Passport Visa Free Countries 2026 — 185+ Destinations

The Australian passport provides excellent global mobility with access to 185+ countries visa-free or with simple online authorisation in 2026. Australians can visit all EU Schengen countries, the UK, USA (ESTA), Japan, South Korea, Singapore, and most of Southeast Asia, the Pacific, and the Americas without a full visa. Australia's strong diplomatic ties across Asia-Pacific give it unique regional advantages: visa-free or easy eVisa access across Indonesia, Thailand, the Philippines, and Pacific Island nations. The passport consistently ranks in the global top 10 for travel freedom.

Pacific Advantage: Australian passport holders enjoy excellent access across the Pacific Islands and Southeast Asia, reflecting strong regional diplomacy.
