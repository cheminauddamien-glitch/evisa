Visa-Free Entry

🏥 Health & Entry Requirements for Uruguay

No mandatory vaccinations. Uruguay has excellent public healthcare (ASSE/mutualistas). Health insurance (mutualista) ~$100-200/month for residents. No COVID-19 requirements.

🕑 Visa Extension & Overstay Rules in Uruguay

90-day stay extendable at Migraciones for additional 90 days. Overstay: fine, must regularize status before departure.

❌ Visa Refusal & Appeals for Uruguay

Very rare for tourists. Uruguay has open immigration policy. Residence straightforward.
