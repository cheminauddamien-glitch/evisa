Do Indonesian Citizens Need a Visa for France?

Yes. Indonesian passport holders must obtain a Schengen Type C visa before travelling to France. There is no eVisa — applications must be submitted in person at the French Embassy in Jakarta or at a VFS Global centre.

Schengen Visa Requirements

The Schengen visa allows stays of up to 90 days in any 180-day period across all 27 Schengen member states. The non-refundable fee is €80 per adult. Apply at least 3 weeks before your travel date.

How to Apply

  1. Register at france-visas.gouv.fr and complete the online form.
  2. Book an appointment at VFS Global or the French Embassy in Jakarta.
  3. Attend in person with all documents; provide biometrics (fingerprints + photo).
  4. Pay €80 and track your application status online.

Required Documents

  • Valid Indonesian passport (min. 2 blank pages)
  • Two recent colour passport photos (35×45 mm, white background)
  • Completed Schengen visa application form
  • Bank statements — last 3 months
  • Travel insurance — min. €30,000 Schengen-wide coverage
  • Confirmed return flight itinerary
  • Proof of accommodation (hotel booking or host invitation)
  • Employment letter or proof of business registration
