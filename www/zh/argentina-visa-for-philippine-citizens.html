Argentine Tourist Visa for Philippine Citizens

Filipinos must apply for an Argentine Tourist Visa (Visa de Turismo) at the Argentine consulate or embassy in their home country before travelling. Processing typically takes 4–8 weeks.

How to Apply for an Argentine Tourist Visa

  1. Locate the nearest Argentine consulate or embassy.
  2. Complete the visa application form (available at the consulate or online).
  3. Gather all required documents (see list below).
  4. Attend a consulate appointment if required.
  5. Pay the applicable visa fee.
  6. Wait for processing — typically 4–8 weeks.
  7. Collect your visa and travel to Argentina.

Required Documents

  • Valid Philippine passport (at least 6 months validity beyond your intended stay)
  • Completed visa application form
  • Passport-sized photographs
  • Bank statements (last 3–6 months) showing sufficient funds
  • Proof of accommodation in Argentina
  • Return or onward flight booking
  • Travel insurance covering the full stay
  • Employment letter or proof of enrollment (for students)

Visa Conditions

  • Fee: Varies
  • Processing: 4–8 weeks
  • Stay: As granted (as determined by immigration)
  • Purpose: tourism only — no work permitted
