🏥 Health & Entry Requirements for Laos

No mandatory vaccinations but Yellow Fever certificate required if coming from endemic area. Hepatitis A/B, Typhoid, Japanese Encephalitis recommended. Malaria prophylaxis recommended for rural areas. No health insurance requirement but strongly recommended. Medical facilities very limited outside Vientiane - medical evacuation to Thailand standard practice.

🕑 Visa Extension & Overstay Rules in Laos

30-day visa extendable at immigration office in Vientiane for $2/day (max 60 additional days). Overstay fine: $10/day. Significant overstays may result in detention. Immigration office: Vientiane, near Morning Market. Processing: 1-3 days for extension.

❌ Visa Refusal & Appeals for Laos

Refusals rare for tourist VOA. May occur for certain nationalities. No formal appeal. Re-entry possible after resolving issues.
