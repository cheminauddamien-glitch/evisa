China eVisa for Australian Citizens (FREE)

Australian passport holders are eligible for China's free eVisa, available at evisa.mfa.gov.cn. The eVisa grants a 30-day stay and is processed within 4 business days. A 72h/144h Transit Without Visa (TWOV) option is also available at select major airports (Beijing, Shanghai, Guangzhou, Chengdu, and others).

eVisa Key Details

  • Fee: Free
  • Stay: Up to 30 days
  • Processing: ~4 business days
  • Apply at: evisa.mfa.gov.cn
  • TWOV: 72h/144h transit without visa at Beijing, Shanghai, Guangzhou and other select airports

How to Apply

  1. Visit evisa.mfa.gov.cn — official Ministry of Foreign Affairs eVisa portal.
  2. Create an account and complete the online visa application form.
  3. Upload a passport photo (white background, recent) and a scan of your passport bio-data page.
  4. Submit — there is no fee for eligible nationalities.
  5. Receive approval by email within 4 business days; print and carry the approval letter.

Required Documents

  • Valid passport — minimum 6 months validity
  • Digital passport photo — white background, recent
  • Scan of passport bio-data page
  • Return or onward flight ticket
  • Hotel booking or accommodation address in China
