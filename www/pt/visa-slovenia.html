🏥 Health & Entry Requirements for Slovenia

Schengen travel insurance EUR 30,000. No mandatory vaccinations. Slovenian ZZZS healthcare for residents. EHIC for EU citizens.

🕑 Visa Extension & Overstay Rules in Slovenia

Schengen 90/180 rule. Register at Administrative Unit within 3 days. Overstay: fine EUR 500-1,200, deportation.

❌ Visa Refusal & Appeals for Slovenia

Standard Schengen grounds. Appeal within 8 days to Ministry of Foreign Affairs.
