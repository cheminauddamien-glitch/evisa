Visão geral das Jordânia Prazos de processamento de visto in 2026

Jordan visa processing is fast. Visa on Arrival at Queen Alia International Airport is typically processed in under 30 minutes upon arrival. eVisa applications submitted online are usually approved within 1–3 business days. JordanPass purchase is instant online. Land border crossings with Israel (Allenby/King Hussein Bridge) operate differently and may require pre-arrangement for certain nationalities. Always check current entry requirements with the Jordanian Ministry of Interior at moi.gov.jo before travel.

Tips to Speed Up Your Jordânia Visa Application

  • Apply well in advance — at least 6–8 weeks before travel for consular visas
  • Ensure all documents are complete, translated (if required), and clearly legible
  • Use the official online portal where available for fastest processing
  • Select priority or express processing if available and your schedule demands it
  • Double-check photo requirements to avoid rejection — see our Requisitos de foto para visto guide
  • Track your application status online using your reference number

What Can Cause Delays?

Common causes of Jordânia visa processing delays include incomplete document submissions, high application volumes during peak travel seasons, additional security or background checks, requests for supplementary information (often adding 1–3 weeks), and technical issues with online portals. Public holidays in Jordan or in the applicant's country can also pause processing. If your application is taking longer than the standard timeframe, contact the embassy or check the official tracking portal. Pre-Travel Checklist

Once your Jordan visa is approved: confirm entry conditions printed on the visa, ensure your passport remains valid for the full duration of your planned stay, carry printed copies of your visa and booking confirmations, check if onward/return tickets must be presented at immigration, and purchase travel insurance covering medical evacuation.
