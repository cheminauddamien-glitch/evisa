🏥 Health & Entry Requirements for Kuwait

Medical exam mandatory for work/residency visa: HIV, Hepatitis B/C, TB, blood tests. Must be done at approved clinics. Health insurance provided by employer for workers. No vaccination requirements for tourists. COVID-19 vaccination not required.

🕑 Visa Extension & Overstay Rules in Kuwait

Tourist visa can be extended once for 90 days at General Department of Residency. Work visa renewal through employer. Overstay fine: KWD 2/day ($6.50). Overstayers may be detained, deported, and face re-entry ban. Must have valid residency to remain in Kuwait.

❌ Visa Refusal & Appeals for Kuwait

Refusals common for single women under 40 traveling alone (escort requirement). Security screening delays for certain nationalities. No formal appeal. Reapplication possible through sponsor.
