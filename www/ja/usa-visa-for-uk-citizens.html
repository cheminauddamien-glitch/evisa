ESTA — Visa Waiver for UK Citizens

UK passport holders are enrolled in the US Visa Waiver Program (VWP), allowing stays of up to 90 days for tourism, transit, or business. An Electronic System for Travel Authorization (ESTA) is required before boarding a US-bound flight or ship.

ESTA is not a visa but an automated security screening. It costs USD 21, is approved within minutes in most cases, and remains valid for 2 years (or until passport expiry).

How to Apply for ESTA in 2026

  1. Visit the official website esta.cbp.dhs.gov.
  2. Complete the online form (passport details, travel information, background questions).
  3. Pay USD 21 by credit or debit card.
  4. Receive authorisation — usually within minutes, sometimes up to 72 hours.
  5. No need to print; your ESTA is linked to your passport electronically.

Documents Required at US Customs

  • Valid UK passport (min. 6 months validity recommended)
  • Approved ESTA authorisation
  • Return or onward ticket
  • Proof of accommodation in the USA
  • Sufficient funds for your stay

Extending Your Stay or Changing Status

ESTA does not allow extensions or changes of immigration status inside the USA. If you need to stay longer than 90 days or wish to work or study, you must apply for the appropriate US visa (e.g., B1/B2, F-1) from your home country before travelling.
