British citizens holding a valid British passport can enter Argentina without a visa for stays of up to 90 days under Bilateral agreement.

Visa-Free Entry to Argentina for British Citizens

British citizens can enter Argentina for tourism, business, or transit without a visa. Present your valid British passport at the port of entry. Ensure your passport has at least 6 months of remaining validity.

Conditions of Visa-Free Entry

  • Maximum stay: 90 days per entry
  • Purpose: tourism, business, transit
  • Passport validity: at least 6 months beyond intended stay
  • Onward/return ticket may be requested at immigration
  • Proof of sufficient funds may be requested

What to Carry on Arrival

  • Valid British passport (6+ months validity)
  • Return or onward flight booking
  • Proof of accommodation
  • Travel insurance (recommended)
  • Proof of sufficient funds
