Cambodia eVisa for Mexican Citizens

Mexican passport holders are eligible for the official Cambodia eVisa (Tourist class), available online at evisa.gov.kh for USD 30. The eVisa grants a 30-day single-entry tourist stay and is processed within 3 business days.

eVisa Key Details

  • Fee: USD 30 (credit/debit card)
  • Validity: Single entry, 30 days stay
  • Processing: 3 business days
  • Entry points: Phnom Penh, Siem Reap, Sihanoukville airports + select land crossings
  • Alternative: Visa on Arrival (USD 35 cash) at the same entry points

How to Apply

  1. Visit evisa.gov.kh — the official Cambodian eVisa portal.
  2. Complete the online application form with passport details and travel dates.
  3. Upload a passport photo (4×6 cm, white background) and passport bio-data page scan.
  4. Pay USD 30 by credit or debit card.
  5. Receive approval by email within 3 business days; print and carry the eVisa approval letter.

Required Documents

  • Valid passport — minimum 6 months validity beyond stay
  • Passport photo — 4×6 cm, white background, taken within 6 months
  • Return or onward flight ticket
  • Hotel booking or accommodation address
  • Credit/debit card for USD 30 payment
