Norway Entry for French Citizens

French passport holders are EU citizens and benefit from the freedom of movement within the European Economic Area. No visa and no ETIAS is needed to enter Norway.

As EU citizens, French and German passport holders enjoy freedom of movement under the EEA Agreement and do not require any visa or ETIAS to enter Norway.

What French Citizens Need

  • Valid passport or national identity card
  • No visa application required
  • No ETIAS required
  • Proof of onward travel or sufficient funds may be requested at border (rarely)

How Long Can You Stay?

As EU citizens, French citizens may stay in Norway for an unlimited period for tourism, work, or study. EU free movement rights apply fully.

Practical Travel Tips

  • Carry a valid EU passport or national ID card
  • Health insurance (EHIC/EHIC successor) is recommended
  • No border formalities within the Schengen Area when arriving from another Schengen country
  • Passport control applies when arriving from outside the Schengen Area
