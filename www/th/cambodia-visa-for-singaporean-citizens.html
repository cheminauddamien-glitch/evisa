Cambodia Visa-Free Entry for Singaporean Citizens

Singaporean passport holders enjoy visa-free entry to Cambodia under the ASEAN Free Movement Agreement. No advance visa application is needed for stays up to 30 days.

Visa-Free Entry Details

  • Fee: Free
  • Stay: Up to 30 days
  • Entry: Present valid passport at immigration
  • Basis: ASEAN bilateral agreement

How to Apply

  1. No advance application is required.
  2. Arrive at Phnom Penh, Siem Reap, or Sihanoukville international airports, or an authorised land crossing.
  3. Present your valid passport at the immigration counter.
  4. Receive a 30-day entry stamp.

Required Documents

  • Valid passport — minimum 6 months validity recommended
  • Return or onward flight ticket
  • Proof of sufficient funds
  • Hotel booking or accommodation address (recommended)
