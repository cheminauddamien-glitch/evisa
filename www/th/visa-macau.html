🏥 Health & Entry Requirements for Macau

No mandatory vaccinations. No health exam for tourists. Work permit requires medical exam. Macau has good public healthcare at Centro Hospitalar Conde de Sao Januario. Health insurance recommended.

🕑 Visa Extension & Overstay Rules in Macau

Visa-free stays generally not extendable. Must exit and re-enter. Work visa renewal through employer 30 days before expiry. Overstay fine: MOP 500/day.

❌ Visa Refusal & Appeals for Macau

Rare for tourists. Work visa depends on labor market test. No formal appeal for tourist entry refusal.
