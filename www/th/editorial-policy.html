Editorial Policy — eVisa-Card.com

At eVisa-Card.com, accuracy and reliability are the foundation of everything we publish. This page explains how our team researches, writes, and maintains visa information to ensure it remains trustworthy and up to date.

Who We Are

eVisa-Card.com is operated by a team of travel and immigration researchers dedicated to providing clear, accurate, and up-to-date visa information for travelers worldwide. Our editorial team monitors official government immigration portals, embassy announcements, and visa policy changes across 48+ countries and 10 languages.

Our Team

Our content is produced by a dedicated visa research team with expertise in international travel regulations, immigration law, and digital publishing. Team members review and update content on a regular basis to reflect the latest policy changes from official sources.

Our Sources

All visa information on eVisa-Card.com is sourced exclusively from official government and embassy websites. We do not rely on third-party aggregators or user-submitted data. Our primary sources include:

  • Official government immigration portals (e.g., USCIS, UK Visas and Immigration, Schengen zone embassies)
  • Ministry of Foreign Affairs portals for each destination country
  • Official eVisa and online application portals (e.g., thaievisa.go.th, eta.cbsa.gc.ca, immi.homeaffairs.gov.au)
  • IATA Travel Centre for airline entry requirement data
  • Official embassy and consulate announcements

Update Frequency

Visa rules change frequently — sometimes overnight. Our editorial process includes:

  • Monthly reviews of all major destination pages
  • Immediate updates when official policy changes are announced (new eVisa launches, fee changes, visa-free agreements)
  • Quarterly audits of all 4,000+ pages across all 10 languages
  • All pages display a Last updated date in the editorial disclaimer at the bottom of each article

Accuracy & Corrections

Despite our best efforts, visa regulations can change rapidly and without prior notice. eVisa-Card.com provides information for reference purposes only. We always recommend verifying current requirements directly with the relevant embassy, consulate, or official government immigration portal before making travel plans.

If you notice outdated or incorrect information on our site, please contact us so we can review and correct it promptly.

Commercial Independence

eVisa-Card.com is editorially independent. Our visa information is not influenced by advertisers, travel agencies, or visa application service providers. We may display advertising (Google AdSense) to support site operations, but advertising relationships have no impact on our editorial content or ratings.

The information on eVisa-Card.com is provided for informational purposes only and does not constitute legal or immigration advice. Visa rules can change at any time without notice.
