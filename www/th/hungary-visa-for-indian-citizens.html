Hungary for Indian Citizens — Overview

Indian passport holders must obtain a Schengen visa (Type C) before travelling to Hungary. The standard fee is EUR 80 and processing takes approximately 15 working days through VFS Global.

Schengen Visa — Key Details

A Schengen short-stay visa (Type C) allows Indian passport holders to visit Hungary and the entire Schengen Area for up to 90 days in any 180-day period.

  • Fee: EUR 80
  • Processing time: approximately 15 working days
  • Apply through: VFS Global
  • Valid for: Schengen Area (26 countries)
  • Maximum stay: 90 days / 180-day period

How to Travel to Hungary

  1. Book an appointment at your nearest VFS Global application centre.
  2. Complete the Schengen visa application form.
  3. Pay the EUR 80 visa fee at the centre.
  4. Submit all required documents (see list below).
  5. Provide biometric data (fingerprints and photo) at the centre.
  6. Wait approximately 15 working days for a decision.
  7. Collect your passport with the visa sticker.

Required Documents

  • Valid passport (issued within the last 10 years, valid for 3+ months beyond intended stay)
  • 2 recent passport-size photos (35×45 mm, white background)
  • Completed Schengen visa application form
  • Travel insurance (min. EUR 30,000 medical cover for the entire Schengen Area)
  • Bank statements (last 3–6 months, showing sufficient funds)
  • Proof of accommodation (hotel bookings or invitation letter)
  • Return/round-trip flight ticket or itinerary
  • Proof of employment (employment letter, payslips, or business registration)
  • If employed: employer's leave approval letter

Frequently Asked Questions

Do Indian citizens need a visa for Hungary?

Yes. Indian passport holders must obtain a Schengen visa before travelling to Hungary. The standard fee is EUR 80 and processing takes approximately 15 working days through VFS Global.

How do I apply for the Schengen visa?

Submit your application through VFS Global in your home country. You will need a valid passport, completed application form, 2 photos, travel insurance, bank statements, proof of accommodation and a round-trip ticket.

How long can Indian citizens stay in Hungary on a Schengen visa?

A standard short-stay Schengen visa allows up to 90 days within any 180-day period across all Schengen member states.
