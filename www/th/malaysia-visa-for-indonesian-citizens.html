Visa-Free Entry to Malaysia for Indonesian Citizens

Indonesian passport holders can enter Malaysia visa-free for up to 30 days under the ASEAN agreement. No visa application or fee is required — present a valid passport at the point of entry.

Entry Requirements

  • Valid passport (minimum 6 months validity beyond intended stay)
  • Return or onward ticket
  • Proof of sufficient funds
  • Accommodation details (may be requested)

Important Notes

  • The visa-free allowance of 30 days is for tourism and short business visits.
  • Extensions are generally not available; depart before your allowed period expires.
  • Working or studying in Malaysia requires separate authorisation.
  • Entry is through all designated international ports of entry in Malaysia.
